Welcome to MTG Deck Builder

To begin, simply register an account to access the search and deck-building functionality. Magic: The Gathering is a trading card game played by two or more people using "decks" typically made of 60 cards. One of the most intriguing parts of the game is the ability to construct decks out of the hundreds of thousands of cards available -- this website replicates the art of deckbuilding while also incorporating additional features such as a public forum.
